ABOUT CELESTIAL

This typeface was inspired by zodiac constellations as well as celestial charts. By combining unique characteristics from both, the typeface imitates the look and feel of authentic constellations. The letterforms are organic and disorganized similar to the nature of stars. The letters are lowercase letters in order to provide more dimension in its ascenders and descenders. Simple in its design, Celestial attempts to represent the beauty and calm of the night sky.
